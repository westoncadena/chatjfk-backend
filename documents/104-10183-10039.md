REASONS. ARRIVED BUENOS AIRES MAY 61 AS CUBAN EXILE AND BECAUSE

MEMBER ANTI-CASTRO GROUP THERE. OTHER INFO AVAILABLE IF DESIRED

BY ROME STATION.
